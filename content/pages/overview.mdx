## Bobu the Bean Farmer

Bobu is a reoccurring character in the Azuki universe. He appears in the third episode of the Azuki Anthology Series and is featured as a Leader card in the Azuki Trading Card Game.

### Personality

Bobu is generally viewed as laidback, wise, and an enjoyer of sake.
